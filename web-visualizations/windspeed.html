Wind Speed


There doesn't seem to be a strong correlation between wind speed and latitude. Most wind speeds do not go above 10 mph.

Wind speed is caused by differences in the atmospheric pressure caused by the uneven heating of the Earth by the sun. Latitude by itself, doesn't predict this. Additional analysis of local geographic features and topography will be helpful in predicting wind speeds.
